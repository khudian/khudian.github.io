Gauging foms



in this text we will fix signs and coefficients in forms

Let $H$ be a Hamiltonian on symplectic manifold $(M,\Omega)$, where in local coordinates $$ \Omega={1\over 2}\omega_{ik}(x)dx^i\wedge dx^k\,, \omega_{ik}=-\omega_{ki}\,,\quad H=H(x)\,. \tag{0.a} $$ Let $\xi=D_H$ be Hamiltonian vector field: $$ \iota_{\xi}\Omega= \Omega {\rfloor}\xi=-dH\,, \tag{0.b} $$ i.e. in local coordinates Hamiltonian vector field $D_H$ defines solutions of differential equations $$ {d x^i(t)\over dt}=\omega^{is}(x){\p H(x)\over \p x^s}\,. \tag{1a} $$ Poisson bracket is $$ \{f,g\}=D_f g\,. $$

Check (0b): $$ \Omega{\rfloor}D_H= {1\over 2}\omega_{ik}(x)dx^i\wedge dx^k {\rfloor} \left( {d x^i(t)\over dt}{\p\over \p x^i} \right) =2\cdot {1\over 2}\omega_{ik}(x) {d x^i(t)\over dt}dx^k= $$ $$ \omega_{ik}(x) \omega^{is}(x){\p H(x)\over \p x^s}dx^k= -\delta_k^s{\p H(x)\over \p x^s}dx^k=-dH\,. $$

Example Suppose $\Omega=dp_i\wedge dq^i$ We claim that $$ D_H= {\p H(p,q)\over \p p_i} {\p \over \p q^i} - {\p H(p,q)\over \p q^i} {\p \over \p p_i}\,, $$ indeed $$ \iota_{D_H}\Omega=dp_i\wedge dq^i {\rfloor} D_H= -{\p H(p,q)\over \p q^i}dq^i- {\p H(p,q)\over \p p_i}dp_i=-dH\,. $$

For Poisson bracket we have $$ \{f,g\}=D_f g= \left[ {\p f(p,q)\over \p p_i} {\p \over \p q^i} - {\p f(p,q)\over \p q^i} {\p \over \p p_i} \right] g(p,q)= {\p f(p,q)\over \p p_i} {\p g(p,q)\over \p q^i} - {\p f(p,q)\over \p q^i} {\p g(p,q)\over \p p_i}\,. $$
