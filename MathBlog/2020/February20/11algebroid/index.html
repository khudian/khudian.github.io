On one generalisation of fundamental vector field



We know how imporatnt are fundamental vector fields.

Today I learnt about an action of `lie algebroid on manifold, i.e. the generalisation of fundamental vector field.

Let $A_1\to A_0$ be Lie algebroid with commutator $[[\,,\,]]$ and with anchor $\a$. Let $M$ be a manifold and $\varphi\colon M\to A_0$.

Definition Let $\rho$ be a map fro sections of Lie algebroid to vector fields on $M$. THis map is called action of Lie algebroid on $M$ if following conditions hold: 1) for every two sections $u,v$ $$ \rho\left(\left[\left[ u,v \right]\right]\right) =\left[\rho(u),\rho(v)\right]\,. $$ 2) Let $f$ be an arbitrary function on the base $A_0$. Then for arbitrary section $u$ $$ \rho (fu)= \varphi^*(f)\rho (u) $$ and $$ \rho (u)\left(\varphi^*\left(f\right)\right) = \varphi^*\left(\a\left(u f\right)\right) $$ $$ $$

In the special case if Lie algebroid is just Lie algebra $$ A_1={\cal G}, A_0=\{*\} $$
