Another formulation of thick morphisms



Let $B_1(M)$ be a space of exact $1$-forms on $M$, abd let $B_1(N)$ be a space of exact $1$-forms on $N$, then an action $S=S(x,q)$ defines the pull-back: $$ B_1(N)\ni g_a(y) dy^a\mapsto f_i(x)dx^i\in B_1(M)\,\, \hbox{such that}\,\, F=\Phi_S^* (G)\,, $$ where functions $F$ and $G$ are potentials of exact $1$-forms $f_i(x)dx^i$ and $g_a(y)dy^a$ $$ \cases { f_i(x)={\p F(x)\over \p x^i}\,, g_a(x)={\p G(y)\over \p y^a}\cr F(x)=G(y)+S(x,q)-y^aq_a\cr y^a(x)={\p S(x,q)\over \p q_a}\,, q_a(x)={\p G(y)\over \p y^a}\,, } $$ Map (1) is well-defined, since $$ F+C=\Phi_S^* (G+C)\,. $$
