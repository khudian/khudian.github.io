Consider Grassmanian $G_1(N)$. For arbitrary two points $P=[\ac]$ and $Q=[\b]$ of this projective space according our studies (Look my MathBlog September 2018) distance between these points is equal to $$ d(P,Q)=\sqrt{ {\rm Tr\,} \left[ 1- NM^+- (MM^+)^{-1} MN^+ (NN^+)^{-1} \right] }= $$ $$ \sqrt{ 1- {(\ac\cdot \b)^2\over \ac^2 \b^2} } =\sin\theta_{\ac,\b}\,. $$ Now define metric at the point $P=[\ac]$ $$ g_{ik}\delta x^i\delta x^k=\sin\theta_{\ac,\ac+\delta \x} =\sqrt{ 1- {(\ac\cdot \left(\ac+\delta \x\right))^2\over \ac^2 \left(\ac+\delta\x\right)^2} }= $$ $$ =\sqrt{ 1- {\left[a_m \left(a^m+\delta x^m\right)\right]^2\over \ac^2 \left(\ac^2+2a_m \delta x^m+\delta_{mn} \delta x^m \delta x^n \right)^2} }= $$ Performing infinitesimal calculations up to the order $2$ we come to $$ g_{ik}\delta x^i\delta x^k= {1\over \ac^2}\left( \delta_{ik}-{a_ia_m\over \ac^2} \right)\delta x^i\delta x^k $$ This is Fubini Studi. $$ X^+ $$
