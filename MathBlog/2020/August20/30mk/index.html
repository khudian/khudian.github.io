Let $S=S(x,g)$ be formal functionali (which is non-linear homomorphism), such that there is another formal functional $P=P_a(x,g)$ such that for variation of functional $S$, $$ {\delta S(x,g)\over \delta g(y)}= P_a(x,g){\p \delta\left(y^a-F^a(x)\right)\over \p y^a}\,, $$ i.e. $$ S(x,g+\vare h)-S(x,g)=P_a(x,g){\p h(y)\over \p y^a}\big\vert_{y^a=S^a(x)}\,. $$ Then $S(x,g)$ is quasilineair functional: $$ S(x,g)=\sum S_n^{a_1\dots a_n}g_{a_1}^*(x)\dots g_{a_n}^*(x)\,, $$ where $$ g_a^*(x)={\p g(y)\over \p y^a}\big\vert_{y^a=F^a(x)}\,. $$
