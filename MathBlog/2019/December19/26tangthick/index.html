Schwarz's question and tangent morphisms



{On the conference in Luxembourg A.S.Schwarz asked a question about properties of thick morphisms which almost coincide with usual maps. Here are calculations initiated by this question}

Let $\Phi-\Phi_S$ be a thick morphism which corresponds to an action $S(x,q)=S(x^i,q_a)$, then For given $g(y)\in C^\infty (M)$ we have $$ f(x)=\Phi_S^*(g(y))=g(y)+S(x,q)-y^aq_a\,, $$ where $y=y^a(x^i),q_b=q_b(x^i)$ are such that $$ \cases { y^a(x)={\p S(x,q)\over \p q_a}\cr q_b(x)={\p g(y)\over \p y^b}\cr }\,. \tag{1} $$ (Here $f(x)$ is a formal expansion series.)

For given function $g=g(y)$ we denote by $y_{(S)}(x)=y^a_{(S)}(x)=y^a_{(g,S)}(x)$, $q^{(S)}(x)=q_b^{(S)}(x)= q_b^{(g,S)}(x)$ formal functions on $x$ such thst $$ \cases { y^a_{g,S}(x)={\p S(x,q)\over \p q_a}\cr q_b^{g,S}(x)={\p g(y)\over \p y^b}\cr }\,. \tag{2} $$ Consider tangent morphism $\Phi_{S(x,q)+\vare (x,q) }-\Phi_{S(x,q) }$ corresponding to the Hamiltonian $h(x,q)$. For arbitrary function $g(y)$ calculate $$ \Phi^*_{S(x,q)+\vare h(x,q) }(g) -\Phi^*_{S(x,q) }(g)\,,\qquad (\vare^2=0)\,. \tag{3} $$ Denote by $y^a_{(\vare)}(x)$, $q_b^{(\vare)}$ functions in equation (2) corresponding to the 'action' (generating function) $S(x,q)+\vare h(x,q)$. \footnote{$^{*}$} {The functions $y^a_{(0)}(x)$, $q_b^{(0)}$ correspond to not perturbed ''point'', thick morphism $\Phi_S$. In particular $$ y^a_{(0)}(x)=\varphi^a(x)+S^{ab}(x)q_b+\dots {\rm with}\,\, q_b={\p g(y)\over \p y_b}\,, $$ where $S(x,q)=S_0(x)+\varphi^a(x)q_a+{1\over 2}S^{ab}(x)q_bq_a+\dots$ } It is useful also the following notation: $$ l_a(y)={\p g(y)\over \p y^a}\,. \tag{4} $$

We have $$ f(x)+\vare H(x)=\Phi_{S+\vare h(x,q)}(g)(x)= g(y)+S(x,q)+\vare h(x,q)-y^aq_a\,, \tag{5} $$ (where $y=y_\vare (x)$, $q=q^{(\vare)(x)}$ are defined by condition (1). We come to $$ f(x)+\vare H(x)=\Phi_{S+\vare h(x,q)}(g)(x)= g(y_\vare(x))+S(x,q^{(\vare)}(x))+\vare h(x,q) -y_{(\vare)}^aq^{(\vare)}_a= $$ $$ g\left((y^a_{(0)}(x)+\vare r^a(x)\right)+ S\left(x,q_a^{(0)}(x)+\vare t_a(x)\right)+\vare h(x,l_a(y_{(0)})) -\left[y_{(0)}^a+\vare r^a(x)\right] \left[q^{(0)}_a+\vare t_a(x)\right]= $$ $$ \underbrace { g\left((y^a_{(0)}(x)\right)+ S\left(x,q_a^{(0)}(x)\right) -y_{(0)}^a q^{(0)}_a }_{\Phi_S^*(g)}+ $$ $$ \vare \underbrace{ \left( {\p g\over \p y_{(0)}^a}r^a +{\p S\over \p q_a^{(0)}}t_a -q^{(0)}_ar^a-y_{(0)}^at_a \right) }_{0} + \vare h(x,l_a(y_{(0)}(x)))\,, $$ i.e. we see that for tangent thick morphism $$ d\Phi_h(x,q)(g)= h \left( x, {\p g(y)\over \p y}\big\vert_{y=y_0(x)} \right) $$ Consider thick morphism which correponds to usual map, i.e. $\Phi=\Phi_S$ with $S=\varphi^a(x)q_a$, and consider properties of the morphism which is "destroy it little bit", i.e. conisder thick morphism
