Thick morphisms and Green function

Fact: action $\cal{S}=\cal{S}(x,y)$ is classics of Green function Let a function $S=S(x,q)$ defines classical thick morphism and $S_\hbar=S_\hbar(x,q)$ defines corresponding quantum thick morphism then $$ G(t,x,y)=\int dq e^{{i\over \hbar}S(x,q)-yq} \tag{1} $$ defines Green function such that $$ \p_tG+\Delta G=0? \tag{1a} $$ The classical analog of formula (1) is the formula $$ W(t,x,y)=S(x,q)-yq \quad {\rm at} \,\, y=S_q\,. $$ It is just classical action.
