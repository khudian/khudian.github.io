Calculate $\Delta (fg)$.

First note that $$ \{f,gh\}= \{f,g\}h+ (-1)^{p(gh)}\{f,h\}g $$ and $$ \Delta_0(f\cdot g)= (\Delta_0 f)\cdot g+ (-1)^{p(f)}f\cdot \Delta_0 g+ (-1)^{p(f)}\{f,g\}\,, \tag{3} $$ and $$ \Delta_0\{f,g\}= \{\Delta_0f,g\}-(-1)^f \{f, \Delta_0 g\}\,. \tag{4} $$ Using the prepresentation (1) one can show that the formulas similar to formulas (3) and (4) hold for operator $\Delta$ also $$ \Delta(f\cdot g)= (\Delta f)\cdot g+ (-1)^{p(f)}f\cdot \Delta g+ (-1)^{p(f)}\{f,g\}\,, \tag{3a} $$ and $$ \Delta\{f,g\}= \{\Delta f,g\}-(-1)^f \{f, \Delta g\}\,. \tag{4a} $$

Now using (3)and (4) calculate $\Delta^2(f)$.

$$ \Delta^2(f)= \Delta \left(\Delta_0(f)+\{A,f\}\right)= $$ $$ \Delta_0 \left(\Delta_0(f)+\{A,f\}\right)+ \left\{A, \left(\Delta_0(f)+\{A,f\}\right) \right\}= $$ $$ \Delta_0^2(f)+ \Delta_0\{A,f\}+ \{A,\Delta_0 f\}+ \{A,\{A,f\}\} $$ Having in mind that $\Delta_0^2=0$ and formula (4) we have $$ \Delta^2 f= \Delta_0^2(f)+ \Delta_0\{A,f\}+ \{A,\Delta_0 f\}+ \{A,\{A,f\}\}= $$ $$: =\{\Delta_0 A,f\}+ \{A,\{A,f\}\} $$ Using Jacobi identity we will rewrite $$ \{A,\{A,f\}\}=-{1\over 2}\{\{A,A\},f\} \tag{5} $$ Indeed $$ (-1)^{(p(f)+1)(p(A)+1)}\{A,\{A,f\}\}+ (-1)^{(p(A)+1)(p(A)+1)} \{A,\{f,A\}\}+ $$ $$ (-1)^{(p(f)+1)(p(A)+1)}\{A,\{A,f\}\}+ \{f,\{A,A\}\}=0\,. $$ i.e. $$ (-1)^{(p(f)+1)} \left( \{A,\{A,f\}\} + (-1)^{p(f)} \{A,\{f,A\}\}+ \{f,\{A,A\}\} \right)=0\,. $$ Since $\{A,f\}=(-1)^f\{f,A\}$ , then we come to equation (5). and we will come to $$ \Delta^2_\rho=\Delta^2 =\{\Delta_0 A,f\}+ \{A,\{A,f\}\} =\{\Delta_0 A,f\}+ {1\over 2}\{\{A,A\},f\} \tag{6} $$ We see that this is vecotr field, moreover it is locally hamiltonian: it has the local hamiltonian $$ H_{\rm local}=\Delta_0A+{1\over 2}\{A,A\} $$ with $A={1\over 2}\rho$
