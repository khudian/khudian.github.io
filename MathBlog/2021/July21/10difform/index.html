Difforms insuper



{More than 40 years ago just before meeting with Manin I realised that there are two definitions of dif-forms.}

One can consider $1$-form as a symbol $r_A(z)dz^A$ One can consider $2$-form as a symbol $\omega_{AB}(z)dz^Adz^B$ such that $$ p(dz^A)=p(z^A)+1 $$ or one can consider $2$-form as a function on tangent vectors, which is superantisymmetrical. These two definitions do not coincide.
