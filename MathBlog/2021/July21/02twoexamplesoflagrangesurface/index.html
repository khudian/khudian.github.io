Ywo examples of lagrange surfaces



Let $E^{2|2}$ be odd symplectic superspace with canonical coordinates $(x,y,\theta,\varphi)$. Consider a function of variables $x,y,\eta$ $$ \Psi(x,y,\eta)=(x^2+y^2-1)\eta \tag{1} $$ and a function $$ \Phi(x,y,\nu)=(x^2+y^2-1)\nu $$ where $\nu$ is an {\rm odd constant}

To the surface $\Psi$ corresponds the $(1|1)$- dimensional Lagrangian surface $$ \L_\Psi\colon\quad {\p\Psi\over \p\eta}=x^2+y^2-1=\,\,, \theta={\p\Psi\over \p x}2x\eta\,, \varphi={\p\Psi\over \p y}=2y\eta\,, $$

To the surface $\Phi$ corresponds the $(2|0)$- dimensional Lagrangian surface $$ \L_\Phi\colon\quad \theta={\p\Psi\over \p x}=2x\nu\,, \varphi={\p\Psi\over \p y}=2y\nu\,, $$ Thus we see that for $(1|1)$-dimensional Lagrangian surface $$ \L_\Psi\colon\quad \cases { x(t,\eta)=\cos t\cr y(t,\eta)=\sin t\cr \theta(t,\eta)=\eta\cos t\cr \varphi(t,\eta)=\eta\sin t\cr }\,\, {\rm dually}\,\, \cases { F_1= x^2+y^2-1=0\cr F_2= y\theta-x\varphi =0\cr }\,, $$ and for $(2|0)$-dimensional Lagrangian surface $$ \L_\Phi\colon\quad \cases { \theta(t,\xi)=2x\nu\cr \varphi(t,\eta)=\eta\sin t\cr }\,\, {\rm dually}\,\, \cases { F_1= \theta-2x\nu\cr F_2= \varphi-2y\nu\cr }\,, $$ where $\nu$ is an odd constant.

Calculate Buttin brackets in both cases

Fur the first case

$$ \{F_1,F_1\} =\{x^2)+y^2-1, x^2+y^2-1\}=0\,, $$ $$ \{F_2.F_2\}=0\,, $$ since $F_2$ is odd function and $$ \{F_1,F_2\}= \{x^2+y^2-1,x\varphi-y\theta\}= {\p F_1 \over \p x} {\p F_2 \over \p \theta}+ {\p F_1 \over \p y} {\p F_2 \over \p \varphi}+ {\p F_1 \over \p \theta} {\p F_2 \over \p x}+ {\p F_1 \over \p \varphi} {\p F_2 \over \p y}= $$ $$ 2x\cdot (-y)+ 2y\cdot (x)+0=0 $$ for the second case $$ \{F_1,F_1\}= \{F_2,F_2\}=0 $$ since both these functions are odd and $$ \{F_1,F_2\}= \{\theta-2x\nu,\varphi-2y\nu\}= {\p F_1 \over \p x} {\p F_2 \over \p \theta}+ {\p F_1 \over \p y} {\p F_2 \over \p \varphi}- {\p F_1 \over \p \theta} {\p F_2 \over \p x}- {\p F_1 \over \p \varphi} {\p F_2 \over \p y}=0 \,. $$
