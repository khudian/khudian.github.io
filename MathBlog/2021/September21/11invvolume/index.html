Baranov-Schwarz transformation



Let $M$ be a supermanifold. One can consider an invariant volume form on it which : $D_{\rm canon.}(x,\theta, dx,d\theta)$: $$ D_{\rm canon.}(x',\theta', dx',d\theta')= D_{\rm canon.}(x,\theta, dx,d\theta) $$ since $$ {\rm Ber} { \p\left( {x',\theta',dx',d\theta'} \right) \over \p\left({x,\theta,dx,d\theta} \right) }=1\,. $$ Example Let $M$ be $p|q$-dimensional supermanifold with local coordinates

$$ \underbrace { \xi^1,\dots,\xi^p }_{\rm even\,\,cordinates}; \underbrace { \eta^1,\dots,\eta^q }_{\rm odd\,\,cordinates} $$ Then local coordinates on $\Pi TM$ are $$ \underbrace { \xi^1,\dots,\xi^p }_{\rm even\,\,cordinates}; \underbrace { d\xi^1,\dots,d\xi^p }_{\rm odd\,\,cordinates}; \underbrace { \eta^1,\dots,\eta^q }_{\rm odd\,\,cordinates} \underbrace { d\eta^1,\dots,\eta^q }_{\rm even\,\,cordinates} $$ If $$ F(x,\theta)=F_\empty(x)+F_i(x)\theta^i+ \dots+F_{\rm top}(x)\theta^1\dots\theta^n $$ and $$ \int F_{\rm top}(x)d^n(x)< \infty $$ then $$ \int F(x,\theta) D_{\rm canon.}(x,\theta, dx,d\theta)= \int F_{\rm top}(x)d^n(x)\,. $$

Let $C=C^{p|q}$ be an arbitrary $p|q$-dimensional surface in superspace $E^{m|n}$: $$ z^A=z^A(\zeta^B), \quad \zeta^B=(\xi^1,\dots,\xi^p,\eta^1,\dots,\eta^q)\,. $$ Then integral of $\Omega$ over $C$ is equal to $$ \int_C\Omega=\int \Omega \left( z(\zeta), {\p z(\zeta)\over \p \zeta} D_{\rm canon}(\xi,d\xi,\eta.d\eta) \right) $$ Define the $p|q$-density, Baranov--Schwarz transform: $$ A^{(BS)}_\Omega \left( z(\zeta)\,, {\p z(\zeta)\over \p \zeta} \right)= \int \Omega\left( z(\zeta)\,, {\p z(\zeta)\over \p \zeta}\bar\zeta \right)D(\bar\zeta) \,. $$ The following theorem takes place (Baranov-Schwarz theorem) $$ \int_C\Omega= A^{(BS)}_\Omega(C)\,. $$
