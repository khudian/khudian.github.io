We have : $$ {3\over 2}=2^x=2^{1\over a_0+{1\over a_1+\dots}} \Rightarrow \left({3\over 2}\right)^{a_0+{1\over a_1+{1\over a_2+\dots}}}=2\,. $$ We have that ${3\over 2}<2<{9\over 4}$, hence $a_0=1$.
Thus we have that $$ {3\over 2}= 2^{1\over 1+{1\over a_1+\dots}}\Rightarrow \left({3\over 2}\right)^{1+{1\over a_1+{1\over a_2+\dots}}}=2 \Rightarrow \left({4\over 3}\right)^{a_1+{1\over a_2+{1\over a_3+\dots}}}= {3\over 2}\,. $$ We have that ${4\over 3}<{3\over 2}<\left({4\over 3}\right)^2$, hence $a_1=1$.
