Let $M,N$ be to (super)manifold) such that $M$ is equipped with measure $d\mu$ and $N$ is equipped with symplectic structure. Let $Q_M$ and $Q_N$ be homological vector fields on $M$ and $N$ respectively such that
1) measure $d\mu$ on $M$ is invariant with respect to $Q_M$
2) symplectic structure on $N$ is invariant with respect to $Q_N$
Then on the space $N^M$ of mapls from $M$ to $N$ one can define:
symplectic structure $\Omega$ THis sympelctic structyure may have opposite parity to sumplectic structure of $N$ and homological vector $\tilde Q_N-\tilde Q_M$, and the Hamiltonian $\cal S$ of this homological vector field obeys the BV equation: $$ ({\cal S},{\cal S})_\Omega= \Omega\left(\X_{\cal S},\X_{\cal S}\right)\equiv 0\,, $$ where $$ \X_{\cal S}\colon \quad \Omega \left(\X_{\cal S}\,,\_\right)=d\Sigma\left(\_\right)\,. $$
