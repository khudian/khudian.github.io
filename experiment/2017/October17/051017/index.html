Linear algebra of thick morphisms


let $X,Y$ be finite-dimensional vector spaces, and let $S\colon X\to Y$ be a linear map. We come to linear map $S^*\colon Y^*\to X^*$ and the bilinear form on the space $X\otimes Y^*$: $$ \y=S(\x),\quad y^\mu=S^\mu_i x^i\,, \tag{1} $$ $$ \p=S^*(\q),\quad p_i=S^\mu_iq_\mu\,, \tag{2} $$ $$ S(\x,\q)=S^\mu_ix^iq_\mu\,. \tag{3} $$
