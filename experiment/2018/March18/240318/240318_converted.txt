   Consider differential equation
    $$
  \hat P (F)=G
    $$
  where $\hat P=\hat P\left({d\over dx}\right)$ polynomial 
on derivative,  $f,g$ polynomials on $x$.
   

{\tt Lemma}:  On the space of polynomials
      $$
  \left({d\over dx}+\lambda\right)^{-1}G(x)=
     G\left(x+{d\over d\lambda}\right){1\over \lambda}\,.
      $$
      

It follows from this lemma that the solutions of 
differential equation
         $$
 \hat P(F)=\prod \left({d\over dx}+\l_i\right)F=G
         $$
is the polynomial
        $$
   F(x)=G\left(x+\sum_i {d\over d\l_i}\right)
      {1\over \l_1\l_2\dots \l_n}\,.
        $$

{\tt Proof of the lemma}

We have that the commutator
$   \left[{d\over dx}+\l,x+{d\over d\l}\right]=0$,
hence
             $$
\left({d\over dx}+\l\right)
           G
         \left(
\left({d\over d\l}+x\right)
         \right)
        {1\over l}
        =   G
         \left(
\left({d\over d\l}+x\right)
         \right)
\left({d\over dx}+\l\right)
      {1\over \l}=  G
         \left(
\left({d\over d\l}+x\right)
         \right)
           {\bf 1}= G(x)\,.
         $$
