We calculate here $$ \Phi^*(t_1 G_1+\dots+t_n G_n) $$ Suppose that $$ S(x,l)=S(x)+\varphi(x)l+{1\over 2}A(x)l^2+ {1\over 6}T(x)l^3+\dots $$ Recall that for an arbitrary $g(y)$ $$ \left[\Phi^*(g)\right](x)=g(y(x))+S(x,l(x))-y(x)l(x)\,, $$ where functions $y(x)=y_g(x), l(x)=l_g(x)$ can be defined from equations $$ y={\p S(x,l)\over \p l}\,,\quad l={\p g(y)\over \p y}\,. $$ We have that for $g\equiv 0$, $\Phi^*(g)=S(x)$, and $$ y_0(x)={\p S(x,l)\over \p l}\big\vert \big\vert\varphi_{l=0}=\varphi(x)\,. $$ We have also that $$ \Phi(g+\vare G)=\vare G(y_g(x))\,. $$ 1 Let $t\colon\quad t^2=0$. (3a) $$ $$ \Phi^*(t_1G_1+t_2G_2+t_3 G_3)= \Phi^*(t_1G_1+t_2G_2)+ t_3G_3\left(y_{t_1G_1+t_2G_2}\right)\,. $$ We have using nilpotency conditions (3a) that $$ y=y_{t_1 G_1+t_2 G_2}=(\varphi+Al+{1\over 2}Tl^2) _{l=t_1G_1'+t_2 G_2'}= $$ $$ \varphi+ A\left(t_1G'_1(y)+t_2G_2'(y)\right)+ {1\over 2}T\left(t_1G'_1(y)+t_2G_2'(y)\right)^2= $$ $$ \varphi+ A\left(t_1G'_1(y=\varphi+\dots)+t_2G_2'(y=\varphi+\dots) \right)+ {1\over 2}T\left(t_1G'_1(\varphi)+t_2G_2'(\varphi)\right)^2 $$ $$ y+ A\left(t_1G'_1(y+At_2G_2'(y))+ t_2G_2'(y+At_1G_1'(y)) \right)+ T(x)t_1t_2G_1'(y)G_2'(y)\big\vert_{y=\varphi(x)}= $$ $$ y+A(x)(t_1G_1'(y)+t_2G_2'(y))+ t_1t_2A^2(x)\left(G_1'(y)G_2(y)'\right)'+ T(x)t_1t_2G_1'(y)G_2'(y) $$ and using (2) we come to $$ \Phi^*(t_1G_1+t_2G_2+t_3 G_3)= \Phi^*(t_1G_1+t_2G_2)+ t_3G_3\left(y_{t_1G_1+t_2G_2}\right)= $$ $$ t_1G_1(y)+ t_2G_2(y)+t_2t_1G_2'(y)A(x)G_1'(y) $$ $$ t_3G_3\left(y+At_1G_1'(y)+t_2G_2'(y))+ t_1t_2A^2\left(G_1'(y)G_2(y)'\right)'+ Tt_1t_2G_1'(y)G_2'(y) \right) $$ $$ t_1G_1(y)+ t_2G_2(y)+t_2t_1G_2'(y)A(x)G_1'(y) \big\vert_{y=\varphi(x)}+t_3G_3(y)+ $$ $$ t_3G_3'\left(At_1G_1'(y)+t_2G_2'(y))+ t_1t_2A^2\left(G_1'(y)G_2(y)'\right)'+ Tt_1t_2G_1'(y)G_2'(y)\right)+ $$ $$ {1\over 2}t_3G_3'' \left(At_1G_1'(y)+t_2G_2'(y)\right)^2 \big\vert_{y=\varphi(x)}\,. $$ Calculating we come to the answer: $$ \Phi^*(t_1G_1+t_2G_2+t_3 G_3)= t_1G_1(y)+ t_2G_2(y)+t_3G_3(y)+ $$ $$ A(x)\left( t_2t_1G_2'(y)G_1'(y)+ t_3t_1G_3'(y)G_1'(y)+ t_3t_2G_3'(y)G_2'(y) \right) + $$ $$ t_3t_2t_1A^2(x)\left( G_1'(y) G_2'(y)G_3'(y) \right)'+T(x)t_3t_2t_1G_1'(y)G_2'(y)G_3(y)' \big\vert_{y=\varphi(x)} \tag{3} $$ Is it possible to recognize $S(x,l)$ by expansion of $\Phi^*(t_mG_m)$?
