Let $\ac,\b$ be two arbitrary non-proportional unit vectors in $\E^2$, and let $\{\e,\f\}$ be an arbitrary orthonormal basis in $\E^2$.
Consider bilinear form: $$ B_P(\x,\y)=\langle P^{-1}\x, P^{-1}\y\rangle_{\E^2} $$ and consider an ellipse $$ B_P(\x,\x)=1\,. $$ This is the image of circle under affine transformation. Its main directions will be eigenvectors of $P$.
Under rotation and affine transformation $x\mapsto ax, y\mapsto by$ it becomes the circle.
