Weyl-Moyal multiplication formula

Let $V$ be $2n$-dimensional symplectic vector space. Then for arbitrary two (smooth) functions on $V$ $$ (f*g)(x)=\exp\left[{1\over \nu} w^{ij} {\p\over \p x} {\p\over \p y} \right] f(x)g(y) \big\vert_{x=y}\,. $$ This is famous Weyl formula for multiplication of Weyl symbols.
