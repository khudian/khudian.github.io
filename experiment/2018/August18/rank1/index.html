On one definition of rank of matrix

Sasha Borovik told me about one extremely helpful definition of the rank of the matrix. Here I will reproduce it.
Let $M=M_{m\times n}$ be $m\times n$ matrix. Then rank of $M$ is the minimal positive number $r$ such that there exist $m\times r$ matrix $L=L_{m\times r}$ and $r\times n$ matrix $R=L_{r\times n}$ such that $$ M_{m\times n}=L_{m\times r}\circ R_{r\times n}\,. $$ We suppose that ${\rm rank\,} M=0$ if all the entries of this matrix vanish.
